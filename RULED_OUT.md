# Ruled Out

Films and shows examined and confirmed to NOT contain the Bride of Frankenstein lightning bolt. This list is organized by decade and is useful for other researchers so they don't duplicate the search effort.

Some entries note the presence of Strickfaden electrical equipment (Tesla coils, etc.) even though the specific lightning bolt was not found.
